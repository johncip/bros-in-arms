Two troopers rush over and knock you out.

You wake later in a hot, sunlit room, with a splitting headache. You've been tied to a table with heavy straps.

Nearby is a horrifying plant that resembles a huge, red venus fly-trap.

“Where am I?” you rasp. A voice answers from behind.

“Ah, you're awake! Welcome to the nursery! We're breeding a new kind of plant—and it's hungry!”

The plant's jaws open slowly, revealing a row of sharp teeth. You'd swear it was smiling.
